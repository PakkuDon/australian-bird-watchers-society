About

Established in March 2017, Australian Bird Watcher's Society is a government owned organisation responsible for tracking the activity of various birds across Australia. We are particularly interested in watching the activity of vulnerable, rare or critically endangered species of birds.

We are currently working with the Department of Environment, Land, Water and Planning to ensure that we can sustain a safe and healthy environment for our feathered friends for years to come.
